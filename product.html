Home Security AI

AI-powered cameras and sensors can intelligently analyze scenes, distinguish between humans, vehicles, and other objects, and trigger alerts for specific anomalies, surpassing the capabilities of traditional security systems. Its algorithms are based on what the technology has been preset with (i.e., the movement of a vehicle in the wrong direction) or what it has learned from a given scene over time. By analyzing visual data, the AI surveillance system can learn to identify objects and recognize patterns that constitute a normal scene.
